90-Day Adoption Playbook

0

Foundation: AI Training & Standards Workshop

Weeks 1-2

Hands-on training focused on prompting best practices. Establish organizational standards for AI tool usage. Identify priority workflows and quick wins. Build shared vocabulary and quality expectations.

1

Discovery & Alignment

Weeks 3-4

Document current workflows and pain points. Select pilot use cases based on training insights. Define success metrics and governance guardrails. Assign workflow owners.

2

Build the Standards Library

Weeks 5-8

Create reusable prompt templates for priority workflows. Develop quality benchmarks and review checklists. Build internal knowledge base of what works. Document organizational prompting standards.

3

Pilot & Measure

Weeks 9-11

Roll out standards to pilot group. Measure time saved and output quality. Iterate based on feedback. Identify candidates for broader rollout.

4

Scale & Sustain

Week 12+

Leadership-ready impact report. Expanded rollout plan. Ongoing governance recommendations. Phase 2 roadmap for advanced use cases.
